Book your tickets now

Theme for 2018:
Eradication of poverty

One of the most challenging problems that our nation is facing is poverty. We strongly feel that robust technological solutions can fight again poverty in the country, than anything else. we the Innovation & Entrepreneurship Development Center (IEDC) of Sahrdaya College of Engineering & Technology, Thrissur, Kerala, in association with Mozilla, ICFOSS and Kerala Startup Mission, is organizing a National Hackathon on " Eradication of poverty".

Instructions

This is a 26 hour contest. Contestants need to brainstorm and arrive at smart solutions for fighting corruption. You can participate individually or in a team of 3-5. The selected teams will be invited to build their ideas into reality. It can be a webapp/website, mobile app or a hardware prototype that you build at the offline event.

Participation Cost?

Participation fee of 1000 per team
Accommodation and food is will be provided!.
